═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

    ↓
Generates 5 research questions
    ↓
Searches web (DuckDuckGo - free!)
    ↓
Scrapes & summarizes each URL
    ↓
Synthesizes all information
    ↓
Generates professional report (Markdown)
    ↓

    ↓
✅ Ready to use/share!
